About SnapTube

SnapTube is a video downloader for Android that launched in November, 2014. Right now it’s available in popular app stores like Opera Mobile Store and UC 9Apps store.

Main features:

  1. Save YouTube videos for offline watching.
  2. Killer music download feature lets you save music videos into MP3 formats in only one step. Save time and space on your phone. No extra encoding process or plugin required.
  3. Search videos with keywords
  4. Download videos via sharing function
  5. Discover new videos with categories
  6. Pause, cancel, or resume a video
  7. Free without any annoying ads
